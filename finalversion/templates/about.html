About Us

WorkBase is a web application designed by Arjun Chandra, Ayush Tripathi, Suhanth Alluri, and Kanishka Verose for the 2021 AlphaHacks Competition. This application serves to help managers view and contact their employees, especially when they are short-staffed. The application was designed within 16 hours. The future plan for this application is to make it larger and more company-based, which allows multiple managers to use the application at once.
